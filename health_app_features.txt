CORE PRODUCT FEATURES

1. Personalized User Profile & Onboarding
- Create health profile (age, sex, height, weight, activity level)
- Dietary preferences (vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
- Allergies & restrictions
- Health goals (fat loss, muscle gain, etc.)
- Auto-calculations:
  - BMI
  - Daily calorie needs
  - Goal-based calorie target
  - Macronutrient ranges

2. Restaurant Discovery Map
- Map with nearby restaurants
- Pins for healthy/goal-compatible options
- Filters:
  - Vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free
  - Low sodium, high protein, low calorie
  - Allergy-friendly
- Restaurant detail card:
  - Name + distance
  - Healthy menu items
  - Personalized recommendation reasoning
  - Nutrition highlights

3. Smart Meal Recommendation Engine
- Suggests what to eat based on:
  - Calorie target
  - Goal (lose/gain/maintain)
  - Activity level
  - Dietary restrictions
  - Time of day
  - Foods already eaten
- Explains why recommendations fit
- Suggests foods to avoid:
  - High sugar
  - High sodium
  - Empty calories
- Non-judgmental explanations

4. Daily Food Logging
- Manual food entry
- Track:
  - Calories
  - Protein, carbs, fats
  - Sodium, sugar
  - Serving size
- Running daily totals
- Compare against targets
- Edit/delete entries

5. Camera-Based Food Recognition
- Scan meals or menus
- Detect:
  - Food type
  - Estimated calories
  - Nutrition info
  - Goal compatibility
- Fast, low-friction UX

6. Fridge / Pantry Scan Mode
- Scan available ingredients
- Outputs:
  - Meal suggestions
  - Snack ideas
  - Simple recipes
- Personalized to goals + restrictions

7. Progress Dashboard
- Visual overview:
  - Calories consumed vs remaining
  - Macro breakdown
  - BMI
  - Weight progress
  - Healthy vs unhealthy trends

8. Healthy Choice Nudges
- Proactive notifications:
  - Healthier nearby options
  - Calorie warnings
  - Snack swaps
  - Hydration reminders

9. Schedule- & Location-Aware Suggestions
- Uses calendar + location
- Pre-meal recommendations
- Quick meal suggestions for busy schedules
- Nearby fast healthy options

10. Meal Quality Scoring
- Ratings:
  - Great choice
  - Good in moderation
  - Avoid for today

11. Saved Preferences & Favorites
- Save:
  - Restaurants
  - Meals
  - Orders
  - Filters

12. Goal-Based Modes
- Modes:
  - Fat loss
  - Muscle gain
  - Maintenance
  - Healthier eating
  - Low sodium / heart health

13. Snack Replacement Suggestions
- Chips → popcorn/protein snacks
- Soda → sparkling water
- Candy → fruit + yogurt

14. Accessibility & Ease of Use
- Minimal typing
- Fast interactions
- Clean mobile UI
